An eclectic at heart and computer scientist by trade

I am a computer scientist currently pursuing my undergraduate engineering degree from Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology in New Delhi, India. Currently a rising senior, I am set to graduate in June 2022 and continue my journey further into the fascinating world of computation by pursuing a master's degree.

I work primarily with Machine Learning and Python but have dabbled in fields far and wide including but not limited to web designing, database management, and IoT. ARTICLES

Publications & Patents

Wireless Sensor Networks(WSNs): Towards an Energy-Efficient Routing Protocol Design

September 2020


This research paper analyses a transmission methodology that enhances the energy characteristics of WSNs. The role I played during this project was primarily associated with running network simulations in MATLAB and technical writing. The article was published in the Turkish Journal of Computer and Mathematics Education.

A Novel Hybrid Clustering Based Transmission Protocol for Wireless Body Area Networks

January 2021


This research paper explores the performance enhancement induced in WSNs by virtue of machine learning applications. It is one of my earliest brainchildren and gave me the privilege f working alongside seasoned, renowned researchers in the field. The research showed promising results and was accepted for publication in the journal Computers, Materials, and Continua.

PINACC: Pin Attack Devive (A Published Indian Patent)

April 2019 - November 2020


PINACC is a women's security provision that was designed during the WiEmpower ideathon organized by the IEEE society's IGDTU chapter. The device is a self-defense injective tool disguised as a hairpin that can help buy women time to escape during a time of crisis. The project was published as a patent by the Intellectual Property Department of India in November 2020.
